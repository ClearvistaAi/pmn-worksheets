Professional Math Nerd  ·  Effective Date: May 3, 2026

Overview: Professional Math Nerd does not operate any servers, databases, or backend infrastructure. We do not collect or receive your personal information. All content within the App is provided for educational and informational purposes only.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes the information practices of Professional Math Nerd ("we," "our," or "us") in connection with the Professional Math Nerd iOS application (the "App"). This Policy applies to all users of the App worldwide.

Please read this Policy carefully. By downloading or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

Data We Control

We do not operate servers, databases, or any backend infrastructure associated with this App. Accordingly, we do not receive, store, or have access to any personal information about you.

The App stores three categories of data locally on your device only:

  • Lesson progress. The App records which lessons you have marked as watched. This data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (Apple UserDefaults) and is never transmitted to us or any other party.
  • Bookmarks. Lessons you bookmark are saved to your device's local storage (Apple UserDefaults) and are never transmitted to us or any other party.
  • Downloaded worksheet files. PDF files you choose to download are saved to your device's local storage. These files remain on your device and are not accessible to us.

Data Collected by Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party services to deliver video content and lesson materials. These services operate independently of us and are governed by their own privacy policies. We do not control what information these services may collect, and we do not receive personal data from them about your usage of the App. You should review the privacy policies of each service below to understand their respective practices.

3. Third-Party Services

YouTube

Video lessons are delivered through an embedded YouTube video player. When you access video content, your device communicates directly with YouTube's servers (operated by Google LLC). YouTube may collect information about that connection, including but not limited to your IP address, device information, and viewing activity, in accordance with its own privacy policy. We are not a party to that data collection and do not receive information from YouTube about your usage. YouTube's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Forms

The App's video request feature opens a Google Form hosted by Google LLC. If you choose to submit a request, any information you enter into the form (such as your requested topic) is transmitted to and processed by Google in accordance with Google's privacy policy. We receive only the content of your form submission; Google may separately collect technical information such as your IP address. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

GitHub

Lesson content and worksheet PDF files are hosted on GitHub. When the App retrieves this content, your device makes network requests to GitHub's servers. GitHub may collect technical information, such as IP addresses and request metadata, in connection with serving these files. We do not control or receive the data GitHub may collect. GitHub's privacy statement is available at docs.github.com.

Apple

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple processes all payment transactions and may collect information in connection with your App Store account and purchase. We do not have access to your payment information or Apple ID. Apple's privacy policy is available at apple.com/legal/privacy.
